Instructions to Download YouTube History Data

  • Go to Google Takeout and sign in with your Google account.
  • Deselect all options.
  • Scroll down to "YouTube and YouTube Music" and select it.
  • Select "All YouTube data included" (if present, otherwise skip).
    Deselect all options.
    Select "history" and click "Ok".
  • Click on "Multiple formats".
    Scroll down to "history".
    Change the file format to JSON and click "Ok".
  • Click on "Next Step".
  • Select "Send download link via email" in the Transfer to option.
    Keep the other options default and click on "Create export". You will receive an email containing your data (usually within 5-10 minutes).
  • In the email, click on "Download your files," sign in to your account, and download the data.
  • Extract the `.zip` file and upload the `watch-history.json` file.

After downloading the file, go back to the home to upload the JSON file.
